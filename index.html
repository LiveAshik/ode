Vitamin D

What it supports:

Red blood cell formation,
DNA synthesis

What form:

Cyanocobalamin (3)

Dosage:

1,000 mcg

Why we included it:

B12 is the most important things nutrient of focus for anyone following a
vegan or plant-based diet. While there are plant based sources of B12
such as certain algae,mushrooms and nutritional yeast, unless vegans
regularly consume foods that are fortified with vitamin B12 studies
have shown that supplements should be included in their daily diet (1).
Clinical evidence supporting B12 supplementation has shown it to be a
completely safe way to prevent or reverse B12 deficiency, any excess
B12 gets excreted through the urine (2).
